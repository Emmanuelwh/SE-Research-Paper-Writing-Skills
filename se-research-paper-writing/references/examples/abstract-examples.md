# Abstract Examples

## Empirical Study Pattern

`Software developers increasingly rely on [practice/tool/data] to [task], yet little is known about [specific phenomenon]. We conducted [study type] on [projects/participants/artifacts] to answer [RQs]. Our results show that [finding 1] and [finding 2]. These findings suggest that [stakeholder] should [implication], while future tools should account for [boundary].`

## Tool/System Pattern

`[SE task] remains difficult because [specific workflow bottleneck]. We present [tool], a [system type] that [core idea]. [Tool] combines [component 1] and [component 2] to support [stakeholder action]. We evaluate [tool] on [subjects/tasks] against [baselines], showing [main result]. The results indicate that [tool] can [practical/research implication] under [scope].`

## LLM4SE Pattern

`Large language models are increasingly used for [SE task], but their reliability under [realistic condition] remains unclear. We study/propose [approach] using [models, tasks, datasets, prompts]. Across [scale], we find that [main result], but [limitation/failure]. These results provide guidance for [deploying/evaluating/designing] LLM-based SE tools.`
