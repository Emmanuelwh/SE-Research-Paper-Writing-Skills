# Introduction Examples

## Problem-First Opening

`Modern software projects rely on [workflow] to maintain [quality attribute]. In this workflow, [stakeholder] must [task] under [constraint]. When [problem] occurs, it can lead to [consequence], making [task] a recurring software engineering challenge.`

## Gap Paragraph

`Prior work has improved [related capability] through [technique family]. However, these approaches typically assume [assumption] or evaluate on [limited setting]. As a result, it remains unclear whether [missing knowledge/capability] holds in [target SE setting].`

## Contribution Paragraph

`This paper addresses the gap by [study/tool/method]. Our key insight is that [insight]. Based on this insight, we [design/conduct/build] [contribution], which enables us to [answer RQs/support task].`

## Results Preview

`Our evaluation on [subjects] shows that [main result]. In particular, [specific finding] suggests [interpretation]. These results support [claim], while also revealing [boundary/failure mode].`
