# Threats to Validity Examples

## Construct Validity

`Our measurement of [construct] relies on [proxy]. This proxy may not capture [missing aspect]. To mitigate this threat, we [validation/inspection/cross-check]. Nevertheless, our conclusions should be interpreted as applying to [bounded construct].`

## Internal Validity

`Observed differences may be affected by [confounder]. We reduce this risk by [control/matching/ablation/fixed protocol]. However, we cannot fully rule out [remaining uncertainty].`

## External Validity

`Our subjects cover [scope], but they may not represent [broader population]. The results may differ for [languages/domains/organizations]. We therefore limit our generality claim to [scope].`

## Conclusion Validity

`The statistical analysis may be affected by [sample size/noise/multiple comparisons]. We report [effect size/confidence interval/correction] to reduce this risk, but the result should be interpreted as [bounded conclusion].`
